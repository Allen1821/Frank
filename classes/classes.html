6010

ASSE 6010 — Medical Gas Installer Course

32 Hours Written + Practical Exam

Course Overview

The ASSE 6010 Medical Gas Installer course provides comprehensive instruction on the installation of medical gas piping systems in healthcare facilities. Students will gain an in-depth understanding of NFPA 99 requirements, proper brazing techniques, and the safety protocols required to work on these critical life-safety systems. The course includes both a written examination and a hands-on practical brazing session.

What You'll Learn

  • Medical gas piping system installation standards
  • NFPA 99 code requirements
  • Brazing techniques and best practices
  • Medical gas equipment installation procedures
  • Testing and verification protocols
  • Safety requirements and documentation
  • Hands-on practical brazing sessions

Who Should Attend

  • Pipefitters and plumbers working in healthcare facilities
  • HVAC technicians transitioning to medical gas work
  • Construction workers specializing in medical facilities
  • Anyone seeking ASSE 6010 certification

⚠ Dress Code Requirements

NO SHORTS OR SANDALS are permitted during the practical brazing session. Students must wear appropriate work attire and closed-toe shoes.

Each student must bring the following brazing materials:

  • 1–1/2″ copper tube, Type L — 50″ long
  • 10 couplings
  • 1 lb filler material

6020

ASSE 6020 — Medical Gas Inspector Course

24 Hours Written Exam

Course Overview

The ASSE 6020 Medical Gas Inspector course is designed for professionals responsible for the inspection and verification of medical gas piping systems. Students will learn how to evaluate systems for compliance with NFPA 99, produce accurate inspection reports, and classify deficiencies according to applicable codes. This course is entirely classroom-based and concludes with a written examination.

What You'll Learn

  • Medical gas system inspection procedures
  • NFPA 99 compliance requirements and verification
  • Documentation and reporting protocols
  • Deficiency identification and classification
  • Code interpretation and application
  • System testing requirements

Who Should Attend

  • State and local building inspectors
  • Healthcare facility engineers
  • Safety officers and compliance managers
  • Quality assurance professionals
  • Anyone responsible for medical gas system oversight

6040

ASSE 6040 — Medical Gas Maintenance Technician Course

32 Hours Written Exam + Demonstration

Course Overview

The ASSE 6040 Medical Gas Maintenance Technician course trains healthcare facility personnel on the proper maintenance, repair, and troubleshooting of in-place medical gas systems. Students will learn NFPA 99 maintenance requirements, how to plan and execute scheduled shutdowns, perform outlet repairs and replacements, and maintain accurate documentation. The course includes a practical demonstration component in addition to the written examination.

What You'll Learn

  • Medical gas system maintenance procedures
  • NFPA 99 maintenance requirements
  • Scheduled shutdown planning and execution
  • Outlet repair and replacement techniques
  • Preventative maintenance protocols
  • Equipment troubleshooting and diagnostics
  • Emergency response procedures
  • Documentation and record keeping

Who Should Attend (Required for)

  • Hospital maintenance staff
  • Healthcare facility engineers
  • Biomedical technicians
  • Plant operations personnel
  • Anyone performing medical gas system maintenance

⚠ Dress Code Requirements

NO SHORTS OR SANDALS are permitted during the demonstration session. Students must wear appropriate work attire and closed-toe shoes.

Each student must bring the following tool:

  • Combination screwdriver
