Regulatory Framework

Standards We Work By

Every service DARPA SOLUTIONS LLC performs is anchored to the regulatory standards that govern healthcare facility safety. Our technicians are trained and certified to the codes that matter most.

NFPA 99

Health Care Facilities Code

The foundational standard for medical gas system design, installation, testing, and ongoing maintenance in healthcare facilities. All DARPA verifications and system evaluations comply with the current edition of NFPA 99.

ASSE 6000

Professional Qualifications Standards

The ASSE 6000 Series defines competency requirements for medical gas personnel including installers, verifiers, maintenance personnel, and instructors. Our team maintains applicable ASSE certifications demonstrating verified industry expertise and professional qualification.
