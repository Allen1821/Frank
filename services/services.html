Regulatory Framework

Standards We Work By

Every service DARPA Solutions performs is anchored to the regulatory standards that govern healthcare facility safety. Our technicians are trained and certified to the codes that matter most.

NFPA 99

Health Care Facilities Code

The foundational standard for medical gas system design, installation, testing, and ongoing maintenance in all healthcare occupancies. All DARPA verifications and installations comply fully with the current edition.

NFPA 55

Compressed Gases and Cryogenic Fluids Code

Governs the storage, handling, and use of compressed gas cylinders, containers, and cryogenic fluid systems directly applicable to liquid oxygen source equipment and high-pressure manifold installations.

ASSE 6000

Professional Qualifications Standards

The ASSE 6000 Series defines competency requirements for medical gas personnel including installers, verifiers, and instructors. Our team holds applicable ASSE certifications to demonstrate proven, third-party–validated expertise.
