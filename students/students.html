Certification Renewal Policy

All ASSE certifications obtained through DARPA Solutions require renewal every 6 months to remain valid. Staying current ensures you are working to the highest standards in medical gas systems and upholds your professional standing in the field.

Renewal Requirements

  • Documentation of continued work in the medical gas field
  • Proof of practical application (brazing or maintenance activities)
  • Completion of renewal forms
  • Payment of applicable renewal fees
Set a reminder 30 days before your renewal date to avoid any lapse in certification.

Your Renewal Roadmap

Follow these four steps to keep your certification active and your career moving forward.

1

Certification Earned

You complete your ASSE course with DARPA Solutions and pass the certification exam.

2

Practice & Document

Six months of active work in the medical gas field keep records of your brazing and maintenance activities.

3

Initiate Renewal

Contact DARPA Solutions 30 days before your expiration date to begin the renewal process.

4

Stay Certified

Submit your documentation and fees to maintain active certification status and protect your professional standing.
